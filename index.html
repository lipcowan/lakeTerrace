Using the Elevator

Our apartment is on the 5th floor and you will need to use the key in the elevator

You will need the key to get to any floors that have apartments ... floors 2 through 5, but you don't need to use the key first to go to the first or lowerlevel floors

Hold the key to the reader and wait for it to turn green, then press the floor you want
