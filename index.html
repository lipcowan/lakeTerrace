Lake Terrace Building Notes

The building is at the corner of 4th Avenue and 59th Street, it's about a mile from our place on 45th Street and is only two blocks from the lake.

Getting into the building should be easy...

Use the black key reader on the righthand side - right of the guest services interface.

First Floor Amenities

  • On the left when you first walk in is the community room with a pool table and TVs - they also have some outdoor seating that is pretty nice
  • Two steps down the hall on your right (past the reception lobby for the leasing office) are the bathrooms, drinking fountain, and apartment gym (which is actually pretty nice)
  • Further down the hallway is the mail area and the parcel room...
    • no need to check the mail BUT...
    • there is a big blue bin that sits there for you to use if you need to carry things up to the apartment - just make sure to return it when you're done
  • Further down there is a long hallway that goes to the northside enterance and some stairs, I use that door when I'm going downtown since it's closer than going out the front
  • FINALLY ... towards the end of the hall are two ELEVATORS!

Using the Elevator

Our apartment is on the 5th floor and you will need to use the key in the elevator

You will need the key to get to any floors that have apartments ... floors 2 through 5, but you don't need to use the key first to go to the first or lowerlevel floors

Hold the key to the reader and wait for it to turn green, then press the floor you want

Second Floor Amenities

Stepping out on your left from the elevators you will see the entrance to the "Terrace" that gives the apartment it's namesake

As of writing this they are still doing minor construction there but it's still really cool to checkout, the chairs and grill are ready but I think they're just working on lights for the evenings

Apartment 513
